My Minecraft Journey

2018 - The Beginning

First Steps in a New World

My very first world! I remember punching trees, building a dirt hut, and barely surviving the first night. Little did I know this was the start of an incredible adventure.

2019 - Learning the Ropes

First Major Builds

Graduated from dirt huts to wooden houses! Learned about farming, mining, and crafting proper tools. Lost my first diamond pickaxe to lava - a rite of passage.

2020 - Adventure Calls

Exploring Beyond

Ventured into the Nether for the first time. Built my first nether portal after many failed attempts. Discovered villages and began trading with villagers.

2021 - Master Builder

Megabase Construction

Started my first megabase project - a floating island city with automated farms and intricate redstone contraptions. Learned about world editing and large-scale planning.

2022 - Community & Multiplayer

Joining Servers

Expanded to multiplayer servers. Joined a survival community and participated in build competitions. Made my first YouTube videos showcasing my builds.

2023 - Present Day

Current Projects

Working on a massive medieval kingdom with fully functional infrastructure. Experimenting with datapacks and mods to enhance gameplay. Documenting everything for the community!
